Retriever online ingest is deployed. The service is exposed on port {{ .Values.service.port }}.

To access from within the cluster:
  http://{{ include "retriever-online.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:{{ .Values.service.port }}

To port-forward and test locally:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "retriever-online.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

Then:
  curl http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.port }}/health
  curl -X POST http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.port }}/ingest -F "file=@document.pdf"
